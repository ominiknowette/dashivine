The real question

Are you ready
for what's next?

David does not usually do this. He does not usually ask like this either. So if you have made it this far, just know he means every part of it.

And now the point of all this

Will you be mine?

I rarely do this, and I do not ask lightly. But I know what I want: something real, something steady, and something that feels like you and me.

I know your heart is not something you hand over lightly. If you ever say yes to love, it would be because you can actually see tomorrow in it, not just the excitement of today. And I will be honest, that kind of depth scares me a little, because this is only my second time stepping into something this real. But maybe that is what makes this matter so much to me, because even with that fear, I still want it to be you.
