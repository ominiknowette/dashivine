How It Started

It all started that day in the corner of PS Hall when I looked to my right and saw the most attractive young lady I had ever seen. I tried to look away, but then, as God would have it, you came and sat close to me. That gave me just enough courage to ask if you could watch over my things, though if I am being honest, I was really just trying to play it cool. You changed something in me in such a short amount of time, and I know I will remember that for a very long time.
